Lesson 8 Overview¶

In this week’s lesson we will be introduced to the fundamental statistical ideas used in many common geological datasets and analyses. This will be our first venture into applying our new Python skills to quantitative geoscience, and we will thus focus more on quantitative and geological concepts, rather than learning new Python skills.
